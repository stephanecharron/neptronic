HVH - Overview

Overview

Complete turnkey Hot Yoga studio system. A fully integrated system that includes ventilation, heating, humidification, energy conservation with closed proportional control, and easy maintenance. Designed to reliably maintain 104°F (40°C) with 50% RH in a space of up to 7060 cu.ft (200m³). Air supply can come directly from the room or from a local duct network.

Features

  • 3-in-1: Heating, ventilation and humidification
  • External/remote temperature/humidity sensor
  • 3-speed fan
  • Built-in thermostat with LCD
    • Displays temperature, humidity and fan status
    • Adjust temperature and humidity setpoints
    • Change filter and system status indications
  • Easy to install and to maintain
  • Optional anti-germ UV light
