Relative Humidity of 35 - 50%
Products Recommended for Application
Building and company owners are interested in keeping humidity levels in the health comfort range of 35-45% RH for their office workers.
The smooth operation of electronic equipment, copy machines, computers, the preservation of expensive wood flooring and the accumulation of static electricity when the air is dry, are all reasons to control humidity at a proper level.
As human beings, we are far less sensitive to dry air than we are to temperature and we commonly fail to associate the discomfort or health problems we experience at work with low relative humidity.
To maintain the RH at the appropriate level, it is necessary to add artificially water to the air using a humidifier.
